Privacy Protocol

Effective Date: May 14, 2026

1. Data Ingestion

WebMaps ingests geographic and business data to provide precise routing and discovery services. We prioritize user anonymity and encrypt all location-based queries using enterprise-grade protocols.

2. Geographic Privacy

Your precise coordinates are only processed locally for map rendering and route calculation. Our servers do not store your persistent movement history unless explicitly authorized for navigation logs.

3. Third-Party Integration

We use OpenStreetMap and Leaflet for mapping. While we do not share your profile data with them, standard tile request headers may be visible to map providers.

WebMaps is committed to the highest standards of data integrity and user sovereignty.
